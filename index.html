Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about our loan services

How quickly can I get my loan approved?

Our advanced system provides instant approval within minutes of application submission. Once approved, funds are transferred to your account within 30 minutes during business hours.

What is the maximum loan amount I can get?

Loan amounts vary by type: Personal loans up to ₦5,000,000, Business loans up to ₦20,000,000, and Emergency funds up to ₦2,000,000. Your eligibility depends on your income and credit profile.

Do I need collateral for a loan?

Most of our personal and emergency loans are unsecured, meaning no collateral is required. For larger business loans, we may require some form of security depending on the amount and terms.

How is the interest calculated?

Our interest rate is 5% per month on the outstanding balance. We use a reducing balance method, which means you pay interest only on the remaining loan amount as you make repayments.

Can I repay my loan early?

Yes! You can repay your loan early without any penalties. Early repayment can help you save on interest charges and improve your credit profile with us.

What happens if I miss a payment?

We understand that financial situations can change. If you anticipate difficulty making a payment, contact us immediately. We offer flexible repayment options and will work with you to find a solution.
